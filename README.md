###  ·  Who

I'm **guanxiaol** — a researcher in the **Claude & ChatGPT developer community** and one of the engineers pushing the **Agent era** forward. I build AI infrastructure, LLM plumbing, and developer tooling — the unglamorous layer that decides whether the AI age becomes everyone's, or just the few who can afford the API bills.

I obsess over **Anthropic's models**, ship in **public**, take design notes from **Apple**, and ride bikes on weekends.

我是 **guanxiaol**，**Claude 与 ChatGPT 开发者社区**里的一位研究员，也是 **Agent 时代**的技术推进者。我做 AI 基础设施、LLM 工具链、开发者工具 —— 那些不光鲜但关键的底层，决定了 AI 红利能否真正落到每一个人头上。

我深度研究 **Anthropic 的模型**，习惯在公开下做事，从 **Apple** 那里学审美，周末上车骑行。

###  ·  Manifesto · 四条信念

> Four convictions I refuse to budge on.

<table>
<tr><td width="48" align="center" valign="top">

**01**

</td><td>

**The cheapest path to the largest dividend.**
The AI age belongs to everyone, not to whoever can write the biggest check to OpenAI. Every tool I ship lowers the floor of access.

**最低的成本，最大的红利。** AI 时代属于每一个人，而不是只属于能给 OpenAI 写最大支票的人。我做的每一个工具，都是把门槛再往下压一截。

</td></tr>
<tr><td align="center" valign="top">

**02**

</td><td>

**Nobody gets left behind.**
If your only blocker to using a frontier model is rate limits, regional pricing, or a card that doesn't pass KYC — that's a bug, and it's solvable. I solve those.

**没有人在这场时代里掉队。** 如果你用不上前沿模型只是因为限额、地区定价、或者一张过不了 KYC 的卡 —— 那是 bug，是可以解的。我就解这个。

</td></tr>
<tr><td align="center" valign="top">

**03**

</td><td>

**Use the best models. Build what matters.**
Claude, GPT, Gemini, DeepSeek, Kimi — pick the strongest for the job, never the cheapest by default. Bad tools waste years; great tools compound forever.

**用最好的模型，做最值得的事。** Claude / GPT / Gemini / DeepSeek / Kimi —— 永远挑最强的，不要默认挑最便宜的。差工具浪费时间，好工具复利一辈子。

</td></tr>
<tr><td align="center" valign="top">

**04**

</td><td>

**Open source is the last weapon against monopoly.**
A few hundred lines of MIT code can do more damage to a moat than a thousand antitrust complaints. So I publish — fast, plain, free.

**开源是对抗垄断最后的武器。** 几百行 MIT 代码对护城河的伤害，比一千份反垄断投诉都大。所以我把东西放出来 —— 快、直接、免费。

</td></tr>
</table>

###  ·  Use freely · 你可以随便用

**All my projects are MIT. You do not need my permission for anything.**
Use them. Fork them. Sell what you build with them. Embed them in your startup. Repackage them. Translate them. Rip out the parts you like and ignore the rest. I don't track, I don't gate, I don't ask. **Go.**

**我所有项目都是 MIT。你不需要我的任何授权。**
随便用、fork、商用、嵌入到你的创业项目、再分发、翻译、只挑你喜欢的那部分 —— 都行。我不追踪、不设门槛、不要求挂名。**去做就是了。**

**One condition — 唯一一个条件:**

> · Don't use them to harm people.
> · Don't use them for fraud, surveillance, manipulation, or extraction at others' expense.
> · Don't use them to break the law.
>
> · 不要用来伤害他人。
> · 不要用来诈骗、监控、操纵或以他人为代价牟利。
> · 不要用来违法。

We're here for fairness, peace, and a less unjust world — not the opposite of those. If you can't sign off on that, please use someone else's library.

我们做这些是为了**公平、和平、和一个更少不公的世界** —— 不是为了反过来。如果你做不到这一点，请用别人的库。

###  ·  Currently · 当下在做

> Building in public, daily.

- 🛡 **[WindsurfPoolAPI](https://github.com/guanxiaol/WindsurfPoolAPI)** — Enterprise multi-account pool serving **113+ AI models** (Claude · GPT · Gemini · Grok · DeepSeek · Kimi) through OpenAI & Anthropic-compatible APIs. One endpoint, zero lock-in. **200+ ⭐**
- 🚴 **[GarminPoster](https://github.com/guanxiaol/garminposter)** — 100% client-side .fit/.tcx/.gpx → poster generator with Coggan-grade NP/IF/TSS/CP/VAM analytics. The free open-source answer to paywalled Relive. *Demo*: <https://guanxiaol.github.io/garminposter/>
- 🧠 **[pomelo-context](https://github.com/guanxiaol/pomelo-context)** — Low-token protocol for agent-readable, human-readable artifacts. Cuts token cost for long-running agents.
- 🤖 **[copilot-feedback-loop](https://github.com/guanxiaol/copilot-feedback-loop)** — VS Code extension that puts humans back in the loop for Copilot Chat Agent. Trust, but verify.
- 🔓 **[windsurf-private-dev](https://github.com/guanxiaol/windsurf-private-dev-archive-windsurfsign-0)** — Zero-cost batch registration tooling. No email, no domain, no friction.
